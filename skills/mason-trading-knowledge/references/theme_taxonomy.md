# Theme Taxonomy

## AI Compute / 国产算力

Keywords: 算力, 国产算力, GPU, HBM, 服务器, 液冷, 算力电源, 中芯国际, 寒武纪, 华为, 英伟达.

Account mapping: 科创50ETF, 通信ETF, 半导体ETF, AI算力产业链 stocks. Avoid chasing extreme high-valuation names after acceleration.

## CPO / Optical Communication

Keywords: CPO, 光模块, 光通信, 光互联, OCS, 光交换, 光纤, 长飞, 亨通, 中际, 新易盛, 天孚.

Account mapping: 通信ETF `515050`, 亨通光电 `600487`, related optical chain leaders. Avoid buying multiple same-bucket names at once.

## PCB / ABF / Carrier Board

Keywords: PCB, ABF, 载板, 玻璃基板, 兴森, 沪电, 生益, 深南.

Account mapping: direct mainboard names if accessible and liquid; otherwise wait for pullbacks. Distinguish industry logic from short-term overheated charts.

## Semiconductor / Materials / Equipment

Keywords: 半导体, 晶圆, 功率器件, 士兰微, 扬杰, 存储, 材料, 设备, 国产化.

Account mapping: 科创50ETF, semiconductor ETFs, selective mainboard names. If user cannot buy `300`/`688`, route to ETFs or mainboard alternatives.

## Small Metals / Nonferrous

Keywords: 小金属, 有色, 稀土, 铋, 锑, 钨, 锂, 锆, 钽, 黄金.

Account mapping: 厦门钨业 and related resources. Check whether China has pricing power; avoid pure rumor spikes.

## Energy / Chemical

Keywords: 能源, 化工, 油价, 原油, 缺电, 电力, 固态电池, 锂电池.

Account mapping: useful when the market style rotates. If the user's chemical ETF is an old weak position and not aligned with current plan, clear rather than average down.

## Macro / Style / Risk

Keywords: 宏观, 策略, 风格, 监管, 关税, 冲突, 市场, 展望, 抄底, 黄金坑, 攻与守.

Account mapping: controls exposure, cash, and whether to chase. Macro/style overrides single-stock excitement.
